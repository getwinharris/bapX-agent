Frequently Asked Questions

What does "free AI compute" mean?

We don't charge for tokens, model calls, or sandbox execution time. Your only cost is the storage your workspace uses. Connect your own API key or OAuth account and the AI compute comes from your existing provider plans.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes — you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my storage?

We'll notify you before you hit your limit. You can upgrade your plan or delete unused files to free up space. No automatic overage charges.

Do I need my own API keys or subscriptions?

Yes — bapX is a platform that connects your existing AI subscriptions (ChatGPT Plus, Claude Pro, etc.) or API keys. You bring your models, we provide the agent platform.
