About bapX

Your AI agent platform — connect your models, unlock your productivity.

Our Mission

bapX exists to give everyone access to a powerful AI agent — without the lock-in. We believe you should own your AI experience: connect your existing subscriptions (ChatGPT Plus, Claude Pro, API keys, and more) and get a unified agent workspace that works the way you want.

No artificial limits, no per-token charges, no feature tiers. Just a platform where your AI works for you, powered by whatever models you choose.

Our Values

🔓 Freedom of Choice

Use any AI provider. Connect via API key, OAuth, or Copilot — we don't lock you into one ecosystem.

🛡️ Privacy First

Your API keys and OAuth tokens are encrypted. Your sandbox is isolated. Your data stays yours.

⚡ Zero Friction

No credit card needed to start. Free compute. Pay only for storage. Get your agent running in minutes.

🧩 Extensible by Design

100+ built-in skills, custom skills from your sandbox, MCP tools — expand your agent's capabilities freely.
