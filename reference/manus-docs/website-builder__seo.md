# Search Engine Optimization

> Built-in SEO for sites created and published on Manus

The built-in SEO feature in Manus is a powerful tool designed to improve your web app's visibility on search engines like Google. It automatically makes your Manus-built websites more search-engine-friendly, helping you attract more organic traffic. The system works by generating a prerendered, static HTML version of your site specifically for search engine bots. This ensures that your content can be easily crawled, indexed, and ranked, while your human visitors continue to enjoy the rich, interactive experience you designed.

## How to Use It

Optimizing your site for search engines is a straightforward process with Manus. Follow these steps to get started:

1. **Enable SEO:** Navigate to your project's Settings, publish your site, and toggle on the SEO feature. This will initiate the automatic prerendering process.
2. **Check Your Score:** Go to the SEO tab in your project dashboard. Here, you'll find a real-time health score for your page and a checklist of important SEO metrics.
3. **Fix with AI:** Instead of manually fixing these issues, you can use the “Optimize with Manus” button. This AI-powered feature will analyze your page content and automatically generate optimized titles, descriptions, and keywords for you.
4. **Identify Issues:** The SEO dashboard will highlight any issues. These might include missing meta descriptions, a lack of keywords, or other on-page SEO problems.
5. **Verify & Rank:** Once the optimizations are applied, you'll see your SEO score improve. Your site is now technically optimized for search engine crawlers.

## What You Need to Know

Our built-in SEO feature comes with several key capabilities to help you succeed:

* Automatic Prerendering: Manus automatically serves a lightweight, fully-optimized HTML version of your site to all major search engine bots, ensuring proper indexing.
* Automated Technical SEO: The system handles critical technical SEO tasks automatically, such as generating canonical URLs, creating a robots.txt file, and maintaining dynamic sitemaps.
* SEO Analysis & Scoring: The SEO dashboard provides a detailed health check for every page, scoring your on-page SEO and offering actionable recommendations.
* AI-Powered Optimization: The “Optimize with Manus” feature uses AI to automatically implement the dashboard’s recommendations, saving you time and effort.

This advanced SEO feature is available as an exclusive benefit for all Manus Members on paid subscription plans.

## Tips

To get the most out of our SEO feature, consider the following tips:

* Submit Your Sitemap: While not strictly required, submitting your sitemap to Google Search Console can significantly speed up the discovery and indexing of your site.
* Use a Custom Domain: A custom domain can improve your branding and may contribute to better SEO performance.
* Regularly Monitor Your SEO Score: Keep an eye on your SEO dashboard for new recommendations and opportunities for improvement.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to be an SEO expert to use this?">
    Not at all. These features are designed to automate the most complex technical aspects of SEO, so you can improve your ranking without being an expert. The dashboard provides simple, clear guidance, and the AI-powered "Optimize with Manus" feature can handle the implementation for you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does this affect my site's performance for regular visitors?">
    It doesn't. The prerendered version of your site is only served to search engine bots. Your human visitors will continue to experience the fast, dynamic version of your site exactly as you designed it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I don’t want my site to be indexed?">
    Manus does not currently support a "Private Publish" option. However, without enabling SEO optimization, your site will be much harder for search engines to discover and index effectively. For restricted access, you can share your project via email with specific visitors or publish it within your team.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
