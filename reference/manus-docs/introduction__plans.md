# Plans and Pricing

> Manus AI operates on a credit-based system that gives you flexibility and control over your usage. Credits are consumed based on the complexity and resources required for each task.

## How Credits Work

The specific number of points/credits consumed by each interaction with Manus depends on the **complexity of the task**.

**Available Plans**

Manus AI offers flexible plans to match your usage needs:

**Free Plan**

Perfect for trying out Manus AI and understanding its capabilities.

Credits: Limited monthly credits Features: Access to core capabilities Best for: Individual users exploring Manus AI

**Pro Plan**

Designed for professionals who regularly use Manus AI for work.

Credits: Generous monthly credit allocation Features: Full access to all capabilities, priority support Best for: Professionals, freelancers, small teams

**Team Plan**

Built for teams that need collaborative access and higher usage limits.

Credits: Shared team credit pool Features: Team collaboration, admin controls, priority support Best for: Growing teams and organizations. \

## Optimizing Credit Usage

Get the most value from your credits with these best practices:

**Be Specific with Your Requests**

Clear, well-defined tasks help Manus AI work more efficiently, reducing unnecessary iterations and credit consumption.

Less efficient: "Research AI" More efficient: "Research the top 5 AI trends in healthcare for 2024 and create a summary report"

**Use the Right Agent Type**

Choose the appropriate agent type for your task. Simple questions don't require the full autonomous agent—use Chat mode for quick queries to save credits.

**Batch Similar Tasks**

When possible, combine related tasks into a single request rather than multiple separate tasks. This reduces overhead and credit consumption.

**Review Intermediate Results**

For complex multi-phase tasks, review intermediate outputs before proceeding. This prevents wasted credits on work that might need adjustment.

## Credit Refills and Add-ons

Need more credits? You have options:

**Monthly Renewal**

Your credit balance automatically refills at the start of each billing cycle based on your plan.

**Credit Add-ons**

Purchase additional credit packs anytime to supplement your monthly allocation. Add-on credits never expire.

**Plan Upgrades**

Upgrade to a higher plan for more monthly credits and additional features.
