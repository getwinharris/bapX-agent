# Desktop

## What it is

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/xtjbiY-tJKk" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" allowfullscreen />

Manus Desktop app brings the power of Manus out of the cloud and directly onto your local computer. Its core capability, My Computer, allows Manus to securely interact with your local files, applications, and development tools, bridging the gap between cloud-based AI and your personal workspace.

This feature is for anyone who wants to automate tasks on their own machine, from simple file management to complex software development. By leveraging your computer's command line, Manus can read and edit files, launch applications, and even use your local hardware like a GPU for intensive tasks. It transforms Manus from a cloud-only agent into a powerful assistant that works alongside you in your own environment.

## How to use it

Getting started with the Manus Desktop app is a straightforward process that connects the AI to your local environment.

1. Download and Install: Get the Manus Desktop app for your operating system (macOS or Windows) from the official Manus website.
2. Sign In: Launch the application and sign in with your Manus account credentials.
3. Connect a Folder: Click on the My Computer tab in the app, then click Add Folder to select a local directory you want Manus to be able to access.
4. Authorize Access: Your operating system will prompt you to confirm and grant access to the selected folder. This ensures you always have control over what Manus can see and do.

## What you need to know

### Security and Control

Your security is our top priority. The My Computer feature is designed to ensure you always have the final say.

**Explicit Approval:** Every command that Manus attempts to execute on your local machine requires your explicit approval. You will be prompted to Allow Once for a single operation or Always Allow for a trusted, recurring task. You are always in command.

**Folder-Scoped Access:** Manus can only access the specific folders you have explicitly authorized. It cannot see or interact with any other part of your file system, giving you granular control over its workspace.

### Availability

The Manus Desktop app with the My Computer capability is available for all users on both macOS and Windows.

### Integration with Your Workflow

My Computer is not a standalone tool; it integrates deeply with your existing Manus workflows.

**Projects:** You can create dedicated Projects that use your local files as a knowledge base

**Scheduled Tasks:** Automate recurring local jobs, such as cleaning up your downloads folder every day or running a script to generate a weekly report, by combining My Computer with Scheduled Tasks.

## Tips

* **Automate Tedious File Management:** Use Manus to batch rename files, sort photos into categorized folders, or organize your project assets. It’s a simple way to save hours of manual work.
* **Unlock Your GPU:** If you have a powerful GPU, you can instruct Manus to use it for demanding tasks like training a machine learning model or running a large language model locally.
* **Build and Test Locally:** Leverage your local development environment to build and test applications. Manus can execute scripts, run compilers, and manage your entire development workflow through the command line.
* **Create a 24/7 Assistant:** Turn an always-on computer, into a dedicated AI assistant. You can assign it complex tasks from any of your devices, anywhere in the world, and it will work on them using its local resources.
